    Delve Deeper: New Tools in Pixlr Designer

    1. Generative Fill

    Ever wanted to add or remove content seamlessly from your images? With our AI-driven Generative Fill, perfection is just seconds away. Imagine you have an image and want to alter a specific area; this tool enables you to do just that. You simply select the part you wish to change and input a prompt, and the AI seamlessly replaces it with your desired content. This not only saves significant time but also ensures that each image you craft is flawless.

    For instance, if you’re working on a cityscape and decide the sky should be dusk instead of noon, just mark the sky area, type in your vision, and watch as the AI transforms it instantly, giving you unparalleled control over your creative endeavors.

    2. Generative Expand

    Pixlr AI Generative Expand is a game-changer for image editing, especially when adapting square photos to different layouts like horizontal or vertical. This innovative tool works by extending your images beyond their initial boundaries, uncovering the hidden portions around your photo. It fills in the extra space intelligently, ensuring that none of the important elements are cropped out.

    Imagine having a beautiful square photo of a beach; with Pixlr AI Generative Expand, you can transform it into a wider landscape format, perfect for a panoramic view, without losing any part of the original scene. This feature is ideal for those who want to repurpose their photos for various formats, offering a seamless and enjoyable photo editing experience.

    3. Backdrop

    The AI Backdrop simplifies the process of removing and changing backgrounds, allowing your products to shine or rejuvenating your photos with a new vibe. Additionally, it’s just a click away to add depth to your pictures, making them more visually appealing.

    Ideal for those who want to elevate their visual content quickly, AI Backdrop offers a transformative experience. For instance, imagine turning a simple product shot into an eye-catching image with an exotic beach backdrop – the possibilities are endless.

    4. Remove Object

    The Remove Object tool allows users to eliminate any undesirable elements, from random people to unsightly blemishes, making it seem as though they were never there. It’s incredibly simple to use, transforming every picture into a masterpiece of perfection without any hassle. Ideal for both professional photographers and everyday photo enthusiasts, AI Remove Object is the ultimate solution for elevating good pictures into great ones.

    For instance, if you have a perfect beach sunset photo disrupted by a stray beach ball, this tool can make it disappear seamlessly, leaving you with a pristine, postcard-worthy image.

    5. Super Scale

    Super-scale offers a remarkable 400% enlargement without compromising on sharpness or clarity. This user-friendly tool is revolutionizing the way we enhance images. Simply upload your photo and let the sophisticated AI take over, ensuring every detail remains crisp and clear.

    For instance, imagine turning a standard smartphone picture into a large, high-quality print, all while preserving its original quality. Give it a try and witness the transformation of your pictures, maintaining their excellent quality, regardless of the size increase!

    6. Generative Transform

    Pixlr’s AI  GnerativeTransform effortlessly isolates subjects from their backdrops while expertly filling in the gaps. With this tool, you can easily resize, pivot, and reposition various elements, granting you the liberty to modify your photos in exciting new ways. Picture this: you have a beach photo, but you want the focus solely on the seashell in the sand.

    Generative Transform allows you to remove distractions, highlighting the shell and making your image compositionally striking. This function not only simplifies photo editing but also injects a dose of creativity and fun into the process, empowering you to reimagine your photographs like never before.

    7. Super Sharp

    This AI tool transforms your blurry images into sharp, detailed photos with ease. It’s perfect for correcting motion blur, whether caused by a shaky hand or a moving object.

    Imagine taking a photo of a fast-moving cyclist that turns out blurry; with AI Super Sharp, that photo can be transformed into a clear, crisp image with every detail of the cyclist and background perfectly visible.

    8. Remove Noise

    AI Remove Noise offers a swift solution for enhancing photos taken in low light or at high ISO settings. It expertly diminishes noise and grain without sacrificing crucial sharpness, turning grainy and blurred images into crisp, detailed pictures.

    This accessible tool is a boon for dealing with tricky lighting situations, enabling even novices to produce professional-standard photographs effortlessly. Its simplicity and effectiveness make it an ideal choice for those aiming for high-quality outcomes with little effort. For instance, a dimly lit birthday party photo can be transformed from a noisy, unclear image into a clear, vibrant memory with just a few clicks.

    9. Remove Background

    Automatically erase backgrounds and eliminate the tedious task of pixel-by-pixel editing using AI Remove Background. Enhance your photos effortlessly by incorporating stylish outlines and shadows.

    This tool is straightforward, efficient, and ideal for adding a professional flair to your images. Experience the transformation firsthand; for instance, a mundane snapshot can swiftly turn into an eye-catching masterpiece.

    10. HDR Filter

    Elevate your photography to a professional standard using our advanced HDR filter. This tool expertly adjusts the balance between light and dark areas in your photos, bringing out the intricate details in both the shadows and highlights.

    Whether you’re capturing the serene beauty of a sunrise or the intricate architecture of a cityscape, our HDR filter ensures that every aspect of your image is enhanced. The result is a photograph that bursts with professional-grade quality, making every shot exceptionally vivid and detailed.

    11. Reflect Effect

    The Reflect Effect provides countless options to morph regular photos into extraordinary, visually appealing works of art. Imagine converting a standard selfie into a piece of symmetrical artwork or transforming a landscape photo into something spellbindingly surreal.

    The Reflect filter is not just an entertaining way to edit photos, but it also adds a personal touch to each creation. It’s an effortless, highly creative process that results in something truly unique. For instance, applying the filter to a city skyline can mirror the image, creating a stunning illusion of a city floating on clouds.
